> Draft — expand from the piflow-enhance skill and the Companion-Mode judging method.

Improving a workflow is the `piflow-enhance` skill. It turns a spotted flaw, a recurring finding, or
human feedback into **one atomic, generalizing change** — capture → route → edit → verify → approve →
commit — never a one-off hack for a single case. It owns the per-node quality bar (the criteria
fixture) and Companion-Mode judging.
